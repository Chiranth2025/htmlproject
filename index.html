Kumar Parvath Trek by Ganesh Tour and Travels

The Kumar Parvath Trek (also called Pushpagiri Trek) is a thrilling 3-day, 2-night expedition into the Western Ghats’ lush forests. At 1,712 m above sea level, Kumar Parvath is Karnataka’s second-highest peak. Our carefully curated itinerary balances challenging ascents, panoramic summit views, and cultural stops, all with safety and comfort in mind.

Highlights
  • Total distance: ~16 km (round trip)
  • Scenic landscapes: shola forests, grasslands, waterfalls
  • Expert local guides and porters
What’s Included
  • Round-trip transport in AC coach or tempo traveller
  • All meals (vegetarian) from dinner Day 1 to lunch Day 3
  • Forest entry permits and camping equipment
